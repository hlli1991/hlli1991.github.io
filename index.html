Haoliang Li

I am an assistant professor at Department of Electrical Engineering, City University of Hong Kong. Before that, I was a Wallenberg-NTU Presidential Postdoc Fellow in the Rapid-Rich Object Search Lab of Nanyang Technological University. I obtained my Ph.D. degree from NTU Singapore supervised by Prof. Alex C. Kot. I am currently serving as an Associate Editor for IEEE Transactions on Information Forensics and Security, and an Action editor for Transactions on Machine Learning Research. Meanwhile, I am serving as Area Chair for some conferences such as NeurIPS, ICLR, CVPR, ICASSP.

haoliang.li[at]cityu.edu.hk

[Google Scholar Profile]
Research

I am broadly interested in AI security, multimedia forensics, and machine learning. My current research focuses on fake news detection, trustworthiness of LLM, transfer learning as well as its application in AI4S, healthcare, circuit design, etc.
