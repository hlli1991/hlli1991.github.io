Haoliang Li

I am an assistant professor at Department of Electrical Engineering, City University of Hong Kong. Before that, I was a Wallenberg-NTU Presidential Postdoc Fellow in the Rapid-Rich Object Search Lab of Nanyang Technological University. I obtained my Ph.D. degree from NTU Singapore supervised by Prof. Alex C. Kot

haoliang.li[at]cityu.edu.hk

[Google Scholar Profile]
Research

I am broadly interested in AI security, multimedia forensics, and machine learning. My current research focuses on secure face recognition, fake news detection, multimedia content forgery detection, transfer learning (domain adaptation/generalization) as well as its application.

Updates

I am looking for motivated PhD/RA with an interest in Efficient AI Hardware Acceleration/AIGC detection. Please feel free to drop me an email if you are interested in working with me. All emails/CVs will be carefully read and evaluated, but only matched candidates will be responded.
